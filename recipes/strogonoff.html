Strogonoff Recipe

Strogonoff Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 4 ounces egg noodles, cooked and drained

How to Make Strogonoff Step-By-Step

  • 1. In a large skillet brown beef in oil over medium high heat.
  • 2. When meat is browned, drain excess fat from skillet. Add garlic powder, salt and pepper and stir in.
  • 3. Add bouillon, onion and mushrooms to skillet and saute until onions are translucent.
  • 4. Remove from heat (very important) and add sour cream.
  • 5. Stir all together and serve over hot cooked egg noodles.
